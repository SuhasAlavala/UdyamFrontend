ABOUT US

UDYAM is the annual techno-management fest of Electronics Engineering Department, IIT BHU, Varanasi.It’s not just these neck-to-neck competitions that you can get at UDYAM. It’s also about the whacky novel contraptions created here that go far beyond the fest. Internships. Patents. All happens. UDYAM sets the stage free for you. Spotlight is on you. Just go with it. We promise after the three days of fest , you will leave with lot of passion to innovate and excel.

UDYAM started in 2010 with the aim of providing a platform for the Indian student community to develop and showcase their technical prowess. For the last 11 years, UDYAM has constantly grown, evolved, and pushed the boundaries of what a branch fest can be. This year marks the 12th Edition of UDYAM, IIT (BHU) Varanasi. Boasting of a huge roster of exciting and engaging events, this edition of UDYAM promises to be grander, greater and more glorious than ever before.

Welcome to UDYAM'21.

EVENTS

MOSAIC

Mosaic is a machine learning and computer vision based event that emphasizes on exposing the learners to the buzzing and state-of-the-art field of artificial intelligence.

Explore More Register

SPYBITS

SpyBits is a cryptography and cryptanalysis based event where participants will learn the art and science behind keeping the information secure from unintended audiences and even how to break into secrets of others by decoding the encoded data.

Explore More Register

I-CHIP

I-Chip is a Verilog HDL based event. The participants are given an exciting problem statement and are expected to code its solution in Verilog, simulate their code and implement it on FPGA board.

Explore More Register

COMMNET

Commnet is a digital communication based event where we explore various components and processes used in digital communication systems such as modulation and coding theory and implement them to solve various challenges using MATLAB.

Explore More Register

CONTINUUM

Continuum is an analog electronics based technical event. The participants get exposure to the simulation of different circuits with desired specifications using a simulation software (usually ORCAD) and hardware implementation of the same.

Explore More Register

DIGISIM

Digisim is a digital electronics based event where the participants get to design optimised digital circuits and their proteus simulations.
